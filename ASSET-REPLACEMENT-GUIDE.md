## 📸 Image Guidelines

### Style & Quality
- **Resolution**: High-resolution (at least 1920px width for desktop images)
- **Format**: WebP for photos (better compression), SVG for logos and icons
- **Style**: Professional, warm tones, natural lighting
- **Theme**: Misty Ooty hills, coffee plantations, premium coffee products

### Color Palette
- Coffee brown: `#3e2723`, `#6f4e37`, `#a67c52`
- Cream/Beige: `#efebe9`, `#d7ccc8`
- Forest green accents: `#5d4037`, `#8d6e63`
- Warm earth tones throughout

### Suggested Image Sources
1. **Stock Photos**: Unsplash, Pexels (search: "coffee plantation", "ooty", "coffee beans", "espresso")
2. **AI Generation**: Midjourney, DALL-E (prompt: "premium coffee plantation in misty hills, cinematic, warm tones")
3. **Custom Photography**: Actual photos from Ooty coffee estates (if available)

## 🎬 Video Guidelines

### Technical Specs
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 minimum
- **Duration**: 5-15 seconds (loops)
- **File Size**: Optimized (under 5MB per video if possible)
- **Style**: Smooth, cinematic, slow-motion where appropriate

### Content Suggestions
- Coffee beans being roasted
- Coffee pouring into a cup (slow motion)
- Steam rising from fresh coffee
- Ooty plantation landscapes with morning mist
- Hands picking coffee cherries
- Coffee brewing process
