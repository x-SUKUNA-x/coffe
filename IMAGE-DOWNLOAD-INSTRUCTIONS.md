## 💡 Quick Tips

1. **Image Size:** Download "Large" or "Original" size from Unsplash
2. **Format:** If Unsplash gives you JPG, that's fine (the code will work with both)
3. **Orientation:** Choose portrait (vertical) images for drink images
4. **Background:** Preferably images with clean/simple backgrounds
5. **Quality:** High resolution (at least 1200px height)
